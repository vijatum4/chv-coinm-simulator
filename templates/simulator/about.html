About

CHV Recovery Simulator · v2.0 · Flask 3.1

Strategy

The CHV (Cascading Hedge & Vindication) Recovery strategy is a systematic approach to trading perpetual futures. It uses ATR-based entry/exit levels and a compounding lot-size recovery scheme to turn whipsaw sequences into profitable cycles over time.

Engine

  • USDM — Standard perpetual futures (e.g. SOLUSDT). P&L settled in USDT. Standard lot × price math.
  • CoinM (Inverse) — Inverse perpetual futures (e.g. DOTUSD_PERP). Contracts priced in USD, settled in base coin. P&L = lots × cs × (1/entry − 1/exit).
  • Backtests pull historical klines from Binance REST API (dapi for CoinM, fapi for USDM).
  • ATR is calculated using Wilder's smoothed method over the configured period.

Infrastructure

  • Flask 3.1 · Python 3.12 · PM2 process chv-simulator
  • Served at port 8503 · Nginx proxy at /sim/
