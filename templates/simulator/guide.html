CHV Recovery Trade Simulator

A systematic simulator for the CHV (Cascading Hedge & Vindication) recovery trading strategy. Configure parameters, simulate individual cycles, run historical backtests, and export bot configs.

🔬 How CHV Works

  • Enter a long or short position with base lots
  • If price hits stop loss → whipsaw: re-enter with 1.5× lots at new level
  • If price hits take profit → cycle wins, P&L calculated
  • Cycle ends at take profit or when capital is exhausted
  • Capital compounds across cycles

⚙️ Key Parameters

ParamDescription
BufferATR efficiency multiplier (0.5–1.0). Controls entry/stop width.
RRReward-to-risk ratio. 2.5 recommended for fee headroom.
Base LotsStarting position size for each cycle.
LeverageExchange leverage. Affects margin, not P&L math.
ATR PeriodATR calculation window. 14 default.

📊 Quick Start

1

Select Market

2

Configure buffer, RR, lots, leverage, and capital in the sidebar.

3

Click "↻ Fetch Live" to auto-fill current price and ATR, or enter manually.

4

Run Backtest

Go to Backtest tab, choose date range, and run. View results, charts and cycle log.

💡 CoinM Notes

  • Contracts are priced in USD, settled in base coin
  • BTC: $100/contract · All others: $10/contract
  • P&L = lots × cs × (1/entry − 1/exit) in base coin
  • Capital shown in base coin (e.g. DOT, SOL, BTC)
  • RR ≥ 2.5 provides fee headroom over breakeven
